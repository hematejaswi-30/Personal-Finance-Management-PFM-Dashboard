## 🚀 Overview
NiveshAI is an advanced **AI-powered Intelligence Suite** designed to serve two primary audiences:
1. **Individuals:** Looking to master their personal finances through AI insights and automated tracking.
2. **Brand Owners:** Needing to manage business finances while analyzing **1,000s of customer reviews** with AI Sentiment Analysis.

## ✨ Key Features

### 👤 Personal Mode
- **Real-Time Wealth Tracking:** Live monitoring of accounts, income, and expenses.
- **AI Financial Advisor:** Context-aware savings tips and spending alerts.
- **Auto-Balance Sync:** Transactions directly impact account totals for 100% precision.

### 🏬 Business Mode (Brand Owner)
- **Review Aggregator:** Unified hub for Amazon, Flipkart, and Google reviews.
- **AI Sentiment Engine:** Classifies thousands of reviews into mood trends in seconds.
- **Smart AI Replies:** One-click automated drafting for professional customer responses.

## 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React with Dynamic Accent Theme Engine.
- **Backend:** Node.js & Express.
- **Database:** MongoDB Atlas (Cloud Cluster).
- **Intelligence:** Groq / Gemini AI Integration.
