Top ways to make this solution impressive
🤖 AI Agent Auto-Responder Game Changer
Don't just classify reviews. Have the LLM automatically draft polite replies addressing the user's specific complaint. The Brand Owner simply clicks "Approve".
Priority: Highest
🚨 Negative Review Alerting Retention
If a 1-star review is detected containing severe keywords ("lawsuit", "dangerous", "broken"), trigger an immediate Email/Slack notification.
Priority: High
📊 Competitor / Topic Mapping Insight
Analyze tags and present visually: e.g., "30% of negative reviews are related to Delivery times". Let the owner fix the root cause.
Priority: High
🛒 Omni-channel Threading UX
Let users reply to an Amazon review, Google review, and Shopify review without ever leaving the dashboard. Fully abstracted architecture.
Priority: Medium
